Starting your first run in **complex furnishings beta** can be overwhelming due to the lack of traditional UI markers and the disorienting nature of the map. Success requires a methodical approach to exploration and a deep understanding of the selling loop.

  <Step num="2" title="Navigating the Stockroom">

  <Step num="3" title="Furniture Collection">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Return Journey">
    This is the most dangerous phase. Retrace your steps back to the storefront. If the layout appears to have changed—a common trick in liminal horror games—do not panic. Look for the most consistent paths and avoid long, open hallways where entities have a clear line of sight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Selling and Upgrading">
    Deposit your items at the sell point. The currency earned can eventually be used for upgrades or utility items that aid in survival. During the beta phase, economy balancing is frequent, so prices for specific furniture types may fluctuate between sessions.
