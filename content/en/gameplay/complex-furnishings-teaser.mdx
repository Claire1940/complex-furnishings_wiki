<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Walkthrough: Your First Shift">

Starting your first run in Complex Furnishings can be overwhelming due to the sheer scale of the environment. The game does not provide a traditional map, forcing you to rely on visual landmarks and your teammates' communication. Success in the early game depends on understanding the "Sell Loop"—the process of entering the stockroom, securing an item, and returning it to the showroom safely.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Showroom Entrance">
    Familiarize yourself with the starting area. This is your safe zone where you can interact with the selling kiosk and communicate with other players before heading into the danger zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Entering the Stockroom">
    Move through the heavy doors into the stockroom. Notice the shift in lighting and sound. Stay close to your teammates, as splitting up is the fastest way to get lost in the infinite corridors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Furniture Identification">
    Look for items that stand out. While everything in the stockroom is technically "furniture," certain pieces have higher values based on their condition and rarity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Extraction">
    Once you have an item, retrace your steps. The stockroom's layout can shift slightly, so use large furniture clusters as landmarks to find the exit doors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalizing the Sale">
    Bring the item back to the showroom kiosk to receive your currency. Use this money to upgrade your carrying capacity or purchase survival tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Beginner Strategy">
For your first few runs, don't go more than three "rooms" deep. It is better to return with a low-value chair than to go missing while looking for a high-value sofa.
</Callout>
