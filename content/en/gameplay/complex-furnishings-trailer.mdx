<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beginner's Guide to the Infinite Stockroom">

Starting your journey after watching the **complex furnishings trailer** can be daunting. The sheer scale of the infinite stockroom is designed to disorient you. To succeed, you need a methodical approach to exploration. Do not treat this like a standard simulator where you can mindlessly grind; treat it like a tactical extraction horror game.

The environment is your biggest enemy. The "liminal" nature of the rooms means they all look similar, which can lead to "looping"—a phenomenon where players accidentally walk in circles until they run out of resources or encounter a threat.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish a Base of Operations">
    Always start by familiarizing yourself with the main storefront. This is your safe zone where you sell items. Identify the primary entrance to the stockroom and never venture too far without a clear mental map of the way back.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mark Your Path">
    Since the game lacks a traditional mini-map, use the furniture itself. Move specific chairs or lamps to corners to act as breadcrumbs. If you see a blue chair you moved earlier, you know you are on the right path home.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Listen for Atmospheric Shifts">
    The audio design in Complex Furnishings is a key survival tool. Sudden silences or changes in the hum of the fluorescent lights often precede an entity encounter. Keep your volume up and avoid loud distractions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Extraction">
    Once your inventory is full or you have secured a heavy item, prioritize the exit. Do not get greedy. The stockroom becomes more dangerous the longer you stay inside a single instance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Entity Awareness">
If you hear footsteps that don't belong to your teammates, stop moving immediately. Some entities in the stockroom are sound-sensitive, and sprinting will only draw them closer to your position.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Tactics and Navigation">

Survival in Complex Furnishings requires more than just running. The **complex furnishings trailer** hints at various threats that inhabit the stockroom. These entities often mimic the stillness of the furniture store, making them difficult to spot until they are already in pursuit. Your best defense is a combination of stealth and spatial awareness.

Navigation is complicated by the "infinite" nature of the map. Rooms may shift or appear to change when you aren't looking. This is a staple of the Backrooms genre and requires players to be adaptable. If a hallway you just walked through is suddenly blocked, look for ventilation shafts or side doors that may have opened.
