### Core Experience Overview

The gameplay loop is deceptively simple: enter the stockroom, find furniture, and bring it back to the storefront to sell. However, as you delve deeper, the "Complex" begins to shift. The infinite stockroom does not want you taking anything, and the further you go, the more aggressive the environment becomes.

<Section icon={Target} title="Getting Started: Beginner's Step-by-Step Guide">

Starting your first run in Complex Furnishings can be overwhelming due to the lack of a traditional map. New players often find themselves lost within minutes of entering the stockroom. Following a structured approach is the best way to ensure your first few furniture hauls are successful without falling prey to the entities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Basic Tools">
    Before entering the stockroom, check the storefront for any available starter gear. While items are limited, ensuring your flashlight is charged is the first priority for visibility in the deeper corridors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Exit Point">
    Always keep the main retail floor entrance in your sightline for the first 50 meters. Use the floor tiles and wall markings as a reference point before the environment turns into the "Infinite" state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Low-Value Furniture">
    In the early game, focus on small chairs and lamps. These are easier to carry and allow you to move faster, which is critical for learning the initial room layouts without being slowed down.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish a Buddy System">
    Never explore alone. Have one player act as a "Scout" (carrying no items) while the other acts as the "Hauler." The Scout's job is to watch for entities while the Hauler focuses on the furniture.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Survival Tip">
If the walls begin to hum louder than usual, drop your furniture and retreat to the last known safe room. This audio cue usually precedes a "Room Shift" where the layout changes entirely.
</Callout>
