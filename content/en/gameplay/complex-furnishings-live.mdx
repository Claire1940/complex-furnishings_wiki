<Section icon={Swords} title="Entities and Hostile Threats">

The stockroom is not empty. While **complex furnishings live** emphasizes atmosphere, entities are a very real threat. These creatures are designed to punish players who move too fast or take too much. Each entity has a specific behavior pattern that you must learn to survive.

| Entity Name | Behavior | How to Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Stocker** | Patrolls aisles, sensitive to sound | Crouch and stay still |
| **The Manager** | Fast chaser, triggered by high value | Drop items and run to light |
| **The Shadow** | Only moves when not looked at | Keep eye contact while backing away |
| **The Static** | Distorts vision and audio | Move to a different room immediately |

Dealing with these threats requires a calm head. If you are spotted, do not panic. The stockroom layout often provides "loops"—circular paths around furniture piles—that you can use to lose a pursuer.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
If the lights turn red, all entities enter an "Aggressive" state. Your only goal at this point is to find a closet or a high-shelf hiding spot and wait for the cycle to end.
</Callout>
