<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Survive Stockroom Entities">

The infinite stockroom is not empty. Hostile entities patrol the corridors, actively hunting players who make too much noise or linger in the darkness for too long. Surviving these encounters is paramount, as dying causes you to drop your current haul and fail your daily sales quota.

### Known Entities & Counterstrategies

* **Captain Clark**: A relentless patrol entity that wanders the stockroom corridors. He has a wide detection radius and will chase you on sight.
  * *Counter*: Break line of sight immediately and hide behind large furniture piles [homepage-modules-2]. Do not attempt to drag furniture while being pursued [homepage-modules-2].
* **Still Life**: A stationary but highly reactive anomaly that challenges players trying to extract nearby high-value items.
  * *Counter*: Move slowly when approaching Still Life territory. Avoid direct eye contact or sudden movements that trigger its aggressive state.

| Entity | Threat Level | Primary Behavior | Detection Method | Best Counter |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Captain Clark** | High | Active patrol, fast chase | Visual sightlines, heavy footsteps | Hide behind partitions, drop heavy cargo to run [homepage-modules-2]. |
| **Still Life** | Medium | Reactive ambush, area denial | Proximity, sudden player movement | Move slowly, avoid looting directly in its line of sight. |
| **The Complex Hum** | Low | Environmental disorientation | Auditory cues, flickering lights | Keep track of physical landmarks; do not rely on sound [homepage-modules-2]. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Drop the Cargo!">
If a chase sequence begins, immediately drop whatever furniture you are dragging [homepage-modules-2]. Your movement speed is severely penalized while hauling, making escape virtually impossible if you try to hold onto your loot.
</Callout>
