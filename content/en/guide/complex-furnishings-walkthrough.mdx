<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Exploration & Selling Guide">

Succeeding in Complex Furnishings requires a systematic approach to exploration, item gathering, and extraction. Rushing blindly into the stockroom is a guaranteed way to trigger a fatal encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Showroom Entrance">
    Familiarize yourself with the starting storefront area. This is your only safe zone and the location of the sales counter. Before opening the stockroom doors, assign roles to your team members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Infinite Stockroom">
    Enter the stockroom slowly. Use large, distinct furniture clusters as physical landmarks. Because the layout mimics classic liminal spaces, rooms can look identical, making mental mapping crucial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identify and Value Furniture">
    Locate high-value props and furniture stock. Smaller, lighter items are easier to carry but yield less cash, while massive items like couches offer high payouts but severely slow down your movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Haul & Sell Phase">
    Carry your items back to the showroom counter. Keep your retreat path completely clear of clutter. If an entity spots you, drop your furniture immediately to regain your maximum sprint speed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Dropping items in narrow corridors can create physical blockages. Ensure you throw furniture to the side if you need to make a quick escape during a chase.
</Callout>

| Entity Type | Danger Level | Behavioral Traits | Best Counter-Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Patroller** | Medium | Moves along set paths, emits low static hums | Crouch-walk to reduce noise and hide behind large shelves. |
