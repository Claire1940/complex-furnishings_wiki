<Section icon={Shield} title="Entity Survival and Chase Mechanics">

The infinite stockroom does not want you taking its inventory [1, 2]. Hostile entities roam the corridors, and their aggression levels spike when you attempt to extract high-value furniture [1, 2]. Understanding how to react during these high-pressure moments is crucial for keeping your character alive.

| Entity Cue | Threat Level | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flickering Lights** | Medium | Stop moving, crouch, and seek immediate cover |
| **Distant Screeching** | High | Identify the nearest open corridor and retreat [2] |
| **Rapid Footsteps** | Critical | Sprint toward recognized landmarks and safe zones [2] |

Survival in Complex Furnishings is about maintaining your composure under pressure [1]. Panic leading to disorganized running will quickly result in a team wipe [2]. 

<Callout type="info" title="Survival Rule">
During a chase, prioritize open pathways over hiding. Entities are fast, but they can be outmaneuvered by taking sharp turns around corners and using large furniture obstacles to break their line of sight [2].
</Callout>
