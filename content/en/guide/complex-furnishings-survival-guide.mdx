<Section icon={Target} title="Survival Tactics: Complex Furnishings Survival Guide for Beginners">

Surviving your first few shifts in the infinite stockroom requires a disciplined approach to exploration. Unlike many Roblox horror games where you can sprint through levels, Complex Furnishings punishes reckless movement. The stockroom reacts to player presence, and "taking" items increases the environmental hostility. Use the following steps to establish a successful salvage routine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish a Base Point">
    Before entering the stockroom, identify the primary exit and selling zone. Ensure all team members know the route back to the showroom.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Slow Progression">
    Move through the stockroom in short bursts. Stop at every doorway to listen for audio cues, such as buzzing fluorescent lights or distant footsteps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Item Prioritization">
    Focus on high-value furniture pieces like couches and wardrobes. Smaller props are easier to carry but offer lower profit margins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Extraction Phase">
    Once you have secured a piece of furniture, the environment may shift. Retrace your steps immediately using the landmarks you identified earlier.
  </Step>
</Steps>
