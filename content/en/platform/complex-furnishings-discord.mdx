<Section icon={Shield} title="Entities and Survival: Avoiding the Stockroom's Wrath">

The stockroom is not just a location; it is a hostile force. As you take furniture, entities—monsters or glitches—will begin to hunt your team. These threats are designed to punish players who stay in one area for too long or carry too much loot at once.

**Survival Tactics for Entities:**
- **Stealth over Speed**: Sprinting creates noise that attracts entities. Move crouched when in deep stockroom areas.
- **Visual Cues**: Watch for shadows that don't match the furniture layout.
- **Co-op Distraction**: If an entity targets a teammate, use the opportunity to clear the path to the exit.
