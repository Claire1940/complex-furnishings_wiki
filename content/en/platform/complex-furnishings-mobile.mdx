To ensure your mobile device runs the infinite stockroom smoothly without dropping frames or lagging during intense chases, follow this optimization sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize Roblox App Settings">
    Open the Roblox settings menu while in-game. Switch the Graphics Mode from Automatic to Manual, and lower the slider to 3 or 4 bars to prevent stuttering in dense stockroom corridors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Touchscreen Sensitivity">
    Locate the Camera Sensitivity slider. Raise it to at least 1.5x of its default value to ensure you can execute quick 180-degree turns when encountering Still Life.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Interface Clutter">
    Minimize any active chat boxes or player lists on your screen. Maximizing your field of view is critical for spotting distant entities in the dim yellow hallways.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enable Performance Stats">
    Turn on the micro-profiler or performance stats in Roblox settings to monitor your ping and frame rate, ensuring a stable connection to the server.
  </Step>
</Steps>
