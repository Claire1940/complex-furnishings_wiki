Carrying heavy items introduces movement penalties, making it harder to escape when things go wrong. Familiarize yourself with the basic inputs below to ensure you do not get stuck in a tight corner.

| Input Key | Action | Context & Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **W, A, S, D** | Move Character | Standard navigation through showrooms and corridors |
| **Shift** | Sprint | Increases speed but drains stamina quickly |
| **E** | Interact / Grab | Pick up furniture, open doors, or search containers |
| **G** | Drop Item | Instantly drop carried furniture to regain full speed |
| **Space** | Jump | Leap over low clutter or navigate blocked pathways |

<Callout type="warning" title="Physics Penalty">
Carrying large furniture pieces like couches or wardrobes significantly reduces your walking and sprinting speed. If you hear a threat approaching, press the drop key immediately to regain your mobility.
</Callout>

For beginners entering the infinite stockroom for the first time, a structured approach is essential. Rushing blindly into the maze will quickly lead to a confrontation with the stockroom's hostile entities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Showroom Exit">
    Locate the main entrance leading from the safe showroom into the stockroom. Before stepping inside, ensure you know the quickest path back to this safety threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Landmark Objects">
    As you enter, look for unique furniture clusters or signs. Use these distinct props to mentally map your route so you do not lose your bearings in repeated rooms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assess Item Value vs. Weight">
    Select items that match your current safety margin. Do not attempt to haul a heavy wardrobe from deep rooms unless your teammates are actively guarding the corridors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute a Co-op Sweep">
    Have one player act as the carrier while the other acts as the navigator and lookout. The lookout should keep a clear path behind the carrier at all times.
  </Step>
</Steps>
