<Section icon={Map} title="Room Navigation: Identifying Liminal Landmarks">

Navigation is the greatest hurdle in **complex furnishings captain clark**. Because the rooms are procedurally influenced and inspired by liminal spaces, players often feel a sense of "déjà vu" that leads to getting lost. To counter this, you must develop a system for marking your path. Since traditional maps are unavailable, environmental storytelling becomes your primary tool.

Pay attention to the specific arrangement of furniture. A room with three identical red chairs is a landmark; a hallway with a single flickering light is a waypoint. If you encounter Captain Clark, your goal should be to lead him away from your established "safe path" so you can circle back once the threat has passed.
