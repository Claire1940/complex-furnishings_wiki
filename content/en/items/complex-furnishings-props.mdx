<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Hauling and Selling Furniture">

Transporting **complex furnishings props** is a multi-stage process that requires coordination, spatial awareness, and quick reflexes. Rushing through the stockroom while carrying heavy furniture is a guaranteed way to get cornered by the entities lurking in the dark.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Area First">
    Before picking up any heavy furniture, clear the immediate rooms and establish a direct path back to the storefront. Look for open hallways and avoid dead ends.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Hauling Roles">
    In co-op play, designate one or two players as the primary haulers. The remaining players should act as scouts, scanning the corridors ahead and behind for any signs of movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Your Stamina">
    Carrying heavy props drains your stamina rapidly. Move in short bursts, dropping the item in a safe corner to recover your stamina before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Use Props as Blockades">
    If an entity begins to chase you, drop your heavy prop in narrow doorways or corridors. This can physically obstruct the entity, giving your team precious seconds to escape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unload and Cash In">
    Deliver the secured furniture directly to the storefront selling zone to instantly claim your cash. Use your profits to purchase utility items for your next run.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
If you are playing solo, stick to medium-weight items. You can move them fast enough to outrun basic entities, ensuring a steady and reliable stream of income.
</Callout>
