Use the step-by-step process below to establish an efficient salvaging routine during your shifts:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Stockroom Entrance">
    Locate the transition door behind your showroom floor. Ensure your flashlight is fully charged before crossing over.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Interactable Furniture">
    Scan the rooms for highlighted or standing furniture pieces. If an item is bolted down or clipping into the environment, ignore it and move deeper.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure and Drag">
    Interact with the object to begin dragging it. Walk backward toward the exit, keeping your camera oriented forward to watch for sudden threats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Process and Sell">
    Bring the item all the way back to the showroom floor. Place it strategically to attract customers, secure the sale, and clear your quota.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Path Clearance Tip">
