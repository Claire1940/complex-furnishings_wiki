<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Evasion Guide">

When Captain Clark or Still Life begins a chase, panic is your worst enemy. Follow this step-by-step evasion process to lose them in the corridors.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Drop Heavy Stock">
    Carrying large items like couches drastically reduces your movement speed. Press the drop key immediately when a chase begins to regain full sprint capability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Break Line of Sight">
    Run around tight corners or zig-zag through shelving units. Do not run in a straight line, as entities can easily catch up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate a Blind Spot">
    Slip behind a large furniture pile or slide into a narrow shelf gap. Hold still and listen to the footsteps to confirm the entity has passed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Regroup and Retrieve">
    Once the footstep sounds fade away, return to the drop point to retrieve your furniture and make a break for the store.
  </Step>
</Steps>
